## Effective date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of June 3, 2026.

## Who operates this site

This site is operated by Jonathan Nishikawa, an independent SEO and marketing consultant based in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada.

**Privacy Officer:** Jonathan Nishikawa
**Contact:** [contact form — to be linked]

## What personal information this site collects

This site is a static publication. It does not run analytics, advertising trackers, or session tracking. The site does not set cookies of its own. Any session or UX cookies that may be created during a visit are set by the visitor's browser and are not under the operator's control.

Personal information is collected only when a visitor voluntarily submits the contact form.

**Categories of personal information collected via the contact form:**

- Name (as provided by the visitor)
- Email address (as provided by the visitor)
- The content of the message submitted

No other personal information is collected. The site does not use Google Analytics or any equivalent third-party analytics service.

## Why this information is collected

Personal information submitted via the contact form is used for:

- Responding to inquiries about consulting services
- Responding to right-of-reply or correction requests under the site's [Permissions, Sources, and Right of Reply policy](/permissions/)
- Operating and improving the consulting service

Personal information is not sold, rented, or used for marketing or advertising purposes.

## Who personal information is shared with

Personal information collected via the contact form is processed by the contact form provider configured for this site, under that provider's published privacy terms. The personal information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside Canada (typically the United States) by that provider. By submitting the contact form, the visitor consents to this transfer.

Personal information is not shared with any other third party except as required by law.

## Safeguards

Personal information is held in the contact form provider's system, accessed only by Jonathan Nishikawa, and reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are applied to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

## Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for a maximum of 24 months unless a consulting engagement begins, in which case retention is governed by the engagement records. Submissions older than 24 months that did not result in an engagement are deleted.

## Your rights

Under the *Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act* (PIPEDA) and, where applicable to Quebec residents, Quebec's *Act respecting the protection of personal information in the private sector* (Law 25), individuals have the right to:

- Access the personal information held about them
- Request correction of inaccurate personal information
- Request deletion of personal information
- Withdraw consent for the use of personal information
- File a complaint with the relevant privacy regulator

Access requests are responded to within 30 days of receipt under PIPEDA. To exercise any of these rights, contact the Privacy Officer via the contact form above.

## Quebec residents

Quebec's Law 25 applies to personal information collected from Quebec residents. This site is published in English only and is not actively marketed in Quebec. Quebec residents who choose to submit the contact form do so under this English-language policy.
